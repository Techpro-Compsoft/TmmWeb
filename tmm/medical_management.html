MEDICAL MANAGEMENT

Trinity Medical Management guarantees ease of operations on-site by offering a stable, easily accessible Medical Management system. We utilize specialized proprietary software to facilitate our operations, which translates into increased efficiency and productivity for our clients and their customers.

Medical Management provides employees on-site with resources that are beyond the standard of care, including:

  • Instant reporting and in-house review of encounters
  • Management of all patient information
  • Tracking of injuries and diseases, medical incidents, and accidents by ICD, CPT, and E codes
  • Cost and outcome analysis
  • Clinic and hospital selection
  • Medical compliance
  • Preferred medical resource identification and usage
  • Transportation of injured
  • Referral management
  • Notification of unexpected outcomes

Trinity Medical Management is a cost-effective solution, due to:

  • Cost savings
  • Reduction in legal claims and expenditures
  • Elimination of lost opportunities
  • Lower Lost Time Accidents
  • Insurance cost effectiveness
  • Positive safety record

Our proprietary Medical Management software system allows Trinity paramedics to quickly and easily manage medical encounters, access board-certified on-call doctors in times of emergency, and facilitate patient care to a hospital or other off-site treatment center.

To assist rig employees in keeping a safe and accident-free workplace, on-site paramedics access our Quick Reference Guide, an electronic database that supplies the latest OSHA standards and other safety guidelines.

Using tools like our Patient Test program, we continually educate and train rig employees on proper safety procedures, which ensures they are as knowledgeable as possible in the event of an emergency situation.

Use of our Medical Management programs results in an improved safety record, allowing our customers increased ability and opportunity to obtain contracts with their customers.
