eVAL

Verifying the skills and knowledge of your employees is now a must.

Offshore – the new mandatory SEMS requirements state that you must make certain of the skills and knowledge of employees to perform the duties to which they have been assigned, consistent with safe work practices.

Onshore – expansion into shale plays requires the constant hiring and training of new employees to meet this demand. Their skills and knowledge must be verified for their safety and that of everyone else!

You could do it yourself, but eVAL is a compelling reason not to!

eVAL FEATURES

  • Electronic competency verification system
  • Verification of skills and knowledge (SEMS SWP requirement)
    • Assist/develop competency modules, if needed
  • Store & access your operations and safety documents on iPads
    • SOPs, SWPs, reference manuals, standards, guidelines, etc.
  • Training and documentation tool
  • iPad based platform
  • Trinity-trained specialist deployed with iPad
  • Secure information – pushed to data warehouse
  • Electronic records with real time reports via web access

eVAL BENEFITS

  • Achieves verifiable regulatory compliance
  • You know what they don't know
  • Obtains leading indicator data and avoids accidents
  • Does not overtax your SMEs (no paperwork)
  • Orderly recordkeeping
  • Lets you prove your employee competency and win business
  • Attacks the greatest cause of accidents – human error
  • Provides peace of mind
