ABOUT US

Founded in 2000.

Refocused in 2003 to shift into an international setting. Since then, we have expanded our services to meet more specialized needs and demands.

Now, with over 10 years in the field we are operating worldwide in some of the most remote locations on earth. We are domestic and international, both on and off-shore.

Our corporate headquarters is in Baton Rouge, LA, but our employees can be found spread across North America, South America, Africa and the Middle East as we continue to expand.

WHO WE ARE

Trinity Medical Management has an astute understanding of the special demands of the remote location industrial workplace and a stellar reputation built on proven experience. All of our staff and on-site personnel are rigorously trained professionals to the highest level of expertise

The TRINITY Mission

Trinity Medical Management is dedicated to helping maximize industrial worksite efficiency and profitability through the intelligent application of on-site health and safety services. We achieve this by implementing pro-active, cost-effective medical programs and safety practices customized to the individual needs of our customers and the unique requirements of each remote industrial location.
